Raj Kashyap

Photographer

Welcome to Raj Kashyap Photography! Based in the historic city of Darbhanga, our studio is the oldest in the city, with a rich legacy spanning over 80 years.

Whether you're looking for stunning studio portraits or vibrant on-site shoots, Raj Kashyap Photography is at your service.

We frequently travel to Noida and Delhi to bring our creative vision to various locations, ensuring every shot tells a unique story.

Equipped with state-of-the-art cameras and advanced photography tools, we deliver exceptional quality and artistic excellence. Our commitment to innovation and detail allows us to create breathtaking visuals that you'll cherish forever. Join us in preserving your special moments with a touch of creativity and sophistication.
